# Camera, Lighting & Look Vocabulary

The **C** (Camera) and **L** (Look) slots of MCSLA are where most prompts win or
lose. Use concrete, filmable language. Pick one option per category — stacking
five camera moves into one shot confuses the model.

## Shot size / framing

| Term | Use it for |
|------|------------|
| Extreme wide / establishing | place the subject in a world |
| Wide | full body + environment |
| Medium | waist-up, conversation |
| Medium close-up | chest-up, emotion |
| Close-up | face, intensity |
| Extreme close-up | eyes, a detail, a product texture |
| Over-the-shoulder | dialogue, POV framing |

## Angle

`eye-level` · `low angle` (powerful, looming) · `high angle` (small, vulnerable) ·
`top-down / overhead` · `dutch tilt` (tension/unease) · `worm's-eye` · `bird's-eye`.

## Camera movement (video)

| Move | Effect |
|------|--------|
| Static / locked-off | stillness, focus |
| Slow push-in / dolly in | rising tension, intimacy |
| Pull-out / dolly back | reveal, isolation |
| Pan (left/right) | follow or scan |
| Tilt (up/down) | reveal scale |
| Tracking / follow | move with the subject |
| Crane / boom | dramatic reveal |
| Handheld | energy, realism, documentary |
| Orbit / arc | showcase a subject or product |
| Whip pan | fast transition |

Keep it to **one primary move** per shot, e.g. "slow push-in," not "push-in while
orbiting and craning up."

## Lens / optics

`14–24mm` ultra-wide (drama, distortion) · `35mm` natural storytelling · `50mm`
human eye, portraits · `85mm` flattering portrait compression · `100mm macro`
detail/product. Add `shallow depth of field` / `deep focus`, `bokeh`,
`anamorphic`, `lens flare` deliberately.

## Lighting

| Style | Mood |
|-------|------|
| Golden hour | warm, nostalgic |
| Blue hour / dusk | cool, melancholic |
| High-key | bright, clean, commercial |
| Low-key / chiaroscuro | dramatic, noir |
| Rembrandt | classic portrait |
| Rim / backlight | separation, silhouette |
| Practical lights (neon, lamps) | grounded, cinematic |
| Soft / diffused | beauty, fashion |
| Hard / direct | edgy, graphic |

## Color & grade

`teal-and-orange` (blockbuster) · `desaturated / muted` (gritty) · `warm amber`
(nostalgic) · `cool steel` (clinical/sci-fi) · `high-contrast B&W` · `pastel`
(dreamy) · `monochrome wash`.

## Film stock / texture

`35mm film grain` · `16mm` · `Super 8 home-movie` · `clean digital` ·
`vintage VHS` · `cinemascope`.

## Putting C + L together

> *...Cold blue-hour light, teal-and-rust grade, 35mm grain. Slow push-in on a
> 35mm lens, low angle.*

One framing, one move, one lens, one lighting idea, one grade, one stock. That's a
complete, unambiguous camera+look spec.
